STUDY OVERVIEW:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This analysis implements the bioinformatics pipeline from:
'Characterization of gene expression profiles in Alzheimer's
disease and osteoarthritis: A bioinformatics study'

METHODOLOGY:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Data: GEO training/validation sets (AD: GSE5281, GSE28146, GSE29378, GSE122063; OA: GSE55235, GSE206848, GSE82107, GSE55457)
2. Quality Control: PCA analysis for batch effects
3. Differential Expression: DEG identification (adj. P < 0.05, |log2FC| > 0.5)
4. Co-DEG Analysis: Identification of common DEGs between conditions
5. PPI Network: Protein-protein interaction network construction
6. Enrichment: GO/KEGG pathway enrichment (Enrichr)
7. Validation: ROC curve analysis for biomarker potential
